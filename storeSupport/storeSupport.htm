Store Support

What is Store Support?

Store Support contains the three outlets stores recieve information and assistance from.

Store Impact Planning ensures that all of our new initiatives are fit to land, ensuring activities can be executed properly. Store Communications provides timely, structured communication to keep the stores informed. Finally, the Store Help Desk provides the stores with a single point of contact when they have questions.

Store Impact Planning

  • Program manager for all store activities
  • Logs and tracks all activities, business initiatives, and strategic projects that impact stores
  • Escalates when any activities are at risk
  • Establishes "Blackout" periods where new initiatives and programs cannot launch
