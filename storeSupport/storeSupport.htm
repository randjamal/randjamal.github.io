Store Support

What is Store Support?

Store Support contains the three outlets stores recieve information and assistance from.

Store Impact Planning ensures that all of our new initiatives are fit to land, ensuring activities can be executed properly. Store Communications provides timely, structured communication to keep the stores informed. Finally, the Store Help Desk provides the stores with a single point of contact when they have questions.

Store Comms

  • One trusted voice for stores
  • Produces useful, engaging, accurate communications to realistic timelines
  • Templates for all activities and communications
  • Works with Merch OPS to ensure communications are accurate and right for stores

Deadlines

Daily Updates (unplanned priority communications): Must be submitted to your Merch Ops partner by 2:30 pm the day prior (Friday by 2:30 pm for Monday).

Department Planner (planned communications): Should be submitted to your Merch Ops partner Wednesday by 2 pm, 14 days prior to the ad break the communication pertains to.
